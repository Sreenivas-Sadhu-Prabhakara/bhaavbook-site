Fabricators

Today's toolkit — and where it snaps

  • WhatsApp sends the number, but it can't tell you who opened it or when to ask again.

A quote nobody follows up on is a customer you paid to find and then let walk away.

Questions

Does my customer need to install anything?

No. You send a link, they tap it and see the quote in their browser. The nudges go out over your own WhatsApp, so it feels like you.

Are the follow-up nudges automatic or do I approve them?

They're prepared for you. At T+2 and T+5 days a ready-to-send nudge lands in your outbox — you glance and send, so the tone always stays yours.

Will it handle GST and different tax rates?

Yes. Add tax per line or on the whole quote; the printable quote page shows a clean tax breakup your customer can trust.

How much does BhaavBook cost?

Pricing is worked out on a quick discovery call and billed monthly on subscription. We're keeping public prices off the page while we onboard our first users.
